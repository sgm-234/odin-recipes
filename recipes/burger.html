Classic Burger Recipe

This classic homemade burger is juicy, flavorful, and easy to make. Perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 250 g ground beef
  • 1 burger bun
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 2 tomato slices
  • 1 onion slice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter (for toasting bun)

Preparation Steps

  1. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper.
  2. Shape the beef into a round patty.
  3. Heat a pan or grill over medium heat.
  4. Cook the patty for 3â€“4 minutes per side.
  5. Add cheese on top and let it melt.
  6. Toast the burger bun lightly with butter.
  7. Assemble the burger with vegetables and patty.

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot with fries and a cold drink.
