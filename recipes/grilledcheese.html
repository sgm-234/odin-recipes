Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A classic grilled cheese sandwich with crispy bread and melted cheese. Simple, comforting, and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • Butter

Preparation Steps

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  4. Cook until the bread is golden brown.
  5. Flip and cook until the cheese melts.

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot with tomato soup or fries.
