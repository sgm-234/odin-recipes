Classic Hot Dog Recipe

This classic hot dog is quick to prepare and perfect for a fast snack or casual meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 hot dog bun
  • 1 sausage (chicken or beef)
  • Mustard or ketchup
  • Chopped onions (optional)
  • Relish (optional)
  • Butter (for toasting bun)

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat a pan or grill over medium heat.
  2. Cook the sausage until evenly browned.
  3. Lightly butter and toast the hot dog bun.
  4. Place the cooked sausage into the bun.
  5. Add mustard, ketchup, or preferred toppings.

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot with chips or fries.
